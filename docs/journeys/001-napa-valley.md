# Drive Elevated Journey No. 001

# Napa Valley

## Your vacation begins the moment you leave the airport.

Most travelers heading to Napa already know where they are staying.

Their hotel is booked.
Their dinner reservations are made.
Their winery tastings are waiting.

But the time between Sacramento and Napa is often treated like dead space.

We see it differently.

That drive is your first chance to slow down, leave the ordinary behind, and let the trip become something more memorable before you ever reach the hotel.

## Begin Slowly

Starbucks is easy.

It is reliable, familiar, and consistent.

But if your schedule allows, we recommend beginning differently.

Temple Coffee Roasters is the kind of stop that changes the pace of the morning. It may not have a drive-thru. It may ask you to park, step inside, and wait a few extra minutes for a properly made coffee.

That is exactly the point.

Order something worth slowing down for. Let the morning breathe a little. Take the coffee with you and let it become the first intentional choice of the journey.

The vacation has already begun.
